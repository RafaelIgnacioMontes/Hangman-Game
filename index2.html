How to Play!

The opponent is trying to summon all 5 pieces of Exodia to defeat you in this game. Guess the word so you can stop him before he obliterates you.

  • You can only guess 1 letter at a time
  • You have a maximum of 4 incorrect guesses before Exodia is summoned
  • Once you win you can press the play again button to get a new word
